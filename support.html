Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the UK. Express shipping options are available at checkout.

What warranty do you offer?

All our PCs come with a 3-year warranty covering parts and labor. Extended warranty options are available.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to most European countries. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location.

Can I upgrade my PC later?

Yes, all our PCs are built with standard components that can be upgraded in the future.
